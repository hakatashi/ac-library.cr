    Overview

    Implements atcoder::mf_graph. Cap is always Int64.

    mf = AtCoder::MaxFlow.new(3)
mf.add_edge(0, 1, 3)
mf.add_edge(1, 2, 1)
mf.add_edge(0, 2, 2)
mf.flow(0, 2) # => 3
